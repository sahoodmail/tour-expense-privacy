This Privacy Policy explains how the app (“the App”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) handles information when you use our mobile application. 1. Overview Tour Expense Manager helps users track travel-related expenses. The App stores data locally on your device and offers optional backup and restore functionality using your personal Google Drive account. We do not operate our own remote servers to store your expense data. 2. Information We Access 2.1 Google Account Information When you sign in using Google authentication, we may access limited profile information such as your name, email address, and Google account identifier. We do not collect or transmit your expense records to external servers owned by us. 2.3 Google Drive Backup Data If backup is enabled, the App uploads a database backup file to your personal Google Drive. Backups are stored inside Google’s appDataFolder and remain under your control. We do not access, view, or retain copies of your backup files. 3. How Information Is Used - Secure user authentication - Enable backup and restore operations - Maintain application functionality 4. The App does not access data unrelated to these functions. 5. Data Storage and Security Primary data remains on your device, and backup files remain in your personal Google Drive. User Control and Data Deletion You may sign out from Google at any time, delete backups from your Google Drive, clear local app data from device settings, or uninstall the App to remove locally stored information. 7.
