Privacy Policy — Tour Expense Manager

Effective Date: 25-02-2026

Overview

Tour Expense Manager helps users track travel-related expenses. All data is stored locally on your device with optional backup to your personal Google Drive. We do not operate remote servers storing your expense data.

Information Accessed

  • Google Account: Used only for secure sign-in.
  • User Expense Data: Stored locally on the device.
  • Google Drive Backup: Stored in your own Google Drive account.

How Data Is Used

  • User authentication
  • User-initiated backup and restore
  • Application functionality

Data Sharing

No personal data is sold or shared. The app uses Google Sign-In and Google Drive APIs only for authentication and backup features.

Data Storage

Expense data remains on your device. Backup files remain inside your personal Google Drive storage.

User Control

You may delete backups, sign out of Google, clear app data, or uninstall the app at any time.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13.
