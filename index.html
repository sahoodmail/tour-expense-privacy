Privacy Policy — Tour Expense Manager

Effective Date: 25-02-2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Android application “Tour Expense Manager”. The app is designed to manage travel expenses while keeping user data primarily on the user’s own device.

Information Accessed

  • Google Account: Used only for secure sign-in.
  • User Expense Data: Stored locally on the device.
  • Google Drive: Optional backup stored in the user’s own Google Drive account.

How Data Is Used

  • User authentication
  • User-initiated backup and restore
  • App functionality

Data Sharing

No personal data is sold or shared. The app only uses Google Sign-In and Google Drive APIs to provide authentication and backup features.

Data Storage

Expense data remains on your device. Backup files remain inside your personal Google Drive storage.

User Control

You can delete backups, sign out of Google, clear app data, or uninstall the app at any time.

Children’s Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13.
