Privacy Policy — Tour Expense Manager

Effective Date: 25-02-2026
Last Updated: 25-02-2026

1. Overview

Tour Expense Manager helps users track travel-related expenses. The App stores data locally on your device and offers optional backup and restore functionality using your personal Google Drive account. We do not operate our own remote servers to store your expense data.

2. Information We Access

Google Account Information: Name, email, and identifier used only for authentication and backup features.

User Expense Data: Stored locally on device. Not uploaded to developer servers.

Google Drive Backup: Stored in your personal Google Drive appDataFolder.

3. How Information Is Used

  • Authentication
  • Backup & Restore
  • App functionality

4. Data Sharing

No data sold. Only Google Sign-In and Google Drive APIs are used for authentication and backup.

5. Data Storage

Data remains on device or your personal Google Drive.

6. User Control

You may delete backups, sign out, or uninstall the app anytime.

7. Children’s Privacy

Not intended for children under 13.
