Privacy Policy — Tour Expense Manager

Effective Date: February 27, 2026

1. Overview

Tour Expense Manager ("the App") helps users track travel-related expenses. All user-entered data is stored locally on your device with an optional backup to your personal Google Drive account. We do not operate remote servers storing your expense data.

2. Information Accessed and Collected

  • Google Account Authentication: Used exclusively for secure sign-in.
  • User Expense Data: Stored locally on your device.
  • Google Drive Backup: Stored solely within your own personal Google Drive account (only accessed if you explicitly initiate a backup).
  • Device and Usage Information (via AdMob): Our third-party advertising partner (Google AdMob) may collect device identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID), IP addresses, and generalized usage data to serve advertisements.

3. How Data Is Used

  • To provide core application functionality (tracking and splitting expenses).
  • To allow user-initiated backup and restore of trip data to your Google Drive.
  • To serve relevant advertisements through the Google AdMob network.

4. Third-Party Advertising & AdMob

Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. To provide and personalize ads, AdMob may automatically collect certain device information, including your IP address, device identifiers, and generalized location data. No personal identifying information (such as your name or email) is shared with advertisers by us.

You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time through your Android device settings (Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization).

For more information on how Google uses this data, please visit Google's Privacy & Terms regarding ads.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your expense data remains securely on your device. Backup files remain securely inside your personal Google Drive storage. We have no access to your Google Drive files other than the specific backup file created by this App.

6. Data Sharing

Your personal data and expense information are never sold, rented, or shared with third parties by the developer. The app only utilizes Google Sign-In, Google Drive APIs, and Google AdMob as described above to provide core services.

7. User Control & Data Deletion

You have full control over your data. You may:

  • Delete your backups directly from your Google Drive account.
  • Sign out of Google within the app settings.
  • Clear the app data or uninstall the app from your device to permanently remove local data.
